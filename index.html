Ahoora Vazifeh art sotry

I am Ahoora Vazifeh (Afsaneh), born in Iran.

The artistic story of my life started when I decided to show my works in a more professional way and showed my first painting called Woman to the public and artists in Nidra Art Exhibition in Istanbul (Turkey).

After that, my passion for painting increased, and the love and passion of my teenage years blossomed again in me, and I became more determined to paint.

In the international exhibition ArtAnkara 2022 (Turkey), I participated with three paintings named Liberation, exhibiting my works alongside Iranian and foreign painters and artists.

After a few months I participated with two works in the ArtContact International Exhibition in Istanbul (Turkey) and recorded sweet and beautiful experiences in my artistic portfolio.

Now I've joined the digital world and will continue my work, creating NFTs.

I will be happy with dear friends and artists who accompany me on this path and support my works.

Thank you, Ahoora
